Our Story

Born in Whitby. Inspired by the streets of Karachi, Kabul, and Toronto.

Not just pizza. Fusion rebellion.

Meezan was born in Whitby to bring the vibrant flavours of Karachi, Kabul, and Toronto together under one roof. We blend authentic Afghan and Pakistani spices with Canadian-style comfort food — creating a fusion that's bold, unforgettable, and 100% halal.

Our kitchen doesn't believe in bland. Every bite is a flavour explosion — from our signature Malai pizzas to our spice-packed Bihari Kebab and Karachi Masala. We're proud to be the hidden gem that Durham raves about.

Halal. Proud. Obsessed with quality. That's the Meezan way. Join the culture.

From Our Kitchen to Your Table

Meezan started with a simple vision: to bring the authentic tastes of South Asia to Whitby in a way that everyone could enjoy. Our recipes are inspired by family traditions, street food culture, and the vibrant fusion of flavours that define Afghan and Pakistani cuisine.

Today, we're proud to be called "the best halal pizza in Durham" by our loyal customers. Whether you're craving a spicy Bihari Kebab pizza, a creamy Afghani Malai, or our famous smash burgers — we've got something for everyone.
